Onboarding

May 31, 2017  · 
1min read

I decided to stay amidst the recent events. First day at work wasn’t as hectic as expected. In fact, it’s laid back and everyone are friendly and interesting. One intern studies UI/UX, which is rare in Hong Kong. I had very nice conversations with him. Hope I could learn a thing or two with him during my time here.

So after today, I decided to build one of my own marketing compaign sites: a comment board with real-time count. It should also display updated comments dynamically. Unlike comments on social media, this comment board could filter out negative content and collect positive feedback. A possible feature is to automatically determine the sentiment of some comments and apply filters. Another feature would be to generate dummy comments based on some sample input.
